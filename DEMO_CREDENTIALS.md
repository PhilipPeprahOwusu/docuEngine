## For Recruiters and Interviewers

This is a Document Intelligence Platform that uses AI agents to analyze contracts, policies, and legal documents. Each demo account represents a different organization with its own isolated data.

**Features to explore:**
- Document upload and management
- AI-powered document analysis (Extract, Risk Assessment, Comparison, Q&A)
- Policy compliance checking
- Multi-agent workflows
- Organization-level data isolation

**Tech Stack:**
- Frontend: Next.js 14, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- Backend: Python FastAPI, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy
- Infrastructure: Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE), Docker, Redis, Qdrant
- AI: Claude 3.5 Sonnet (Anthropic)
